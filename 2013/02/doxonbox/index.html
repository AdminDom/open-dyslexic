DoxOnBox

DoxOnBox with OpenDyslexic and Beeline viewing Wikipedia

DoxOnBox uses a combination of linguistic tagging, Beelinereader and OpenDyslexic to make content on your iPad more readable. You can pull up articles from Wikipedia or books from your Box account and have them displayed according to your settings. It’s open source also, so you can easily contribute to it or learn from it.

Get it:

  • https://github.com/snarshad/doxonbox (Source)
  • git://github.com/snarshad/doxonbox.git (Clone)
