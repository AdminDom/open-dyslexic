Clicker

Clicker 6 with OpenDyslexic

Clicker is word processor that helps children learn to read and write. It has many unique features, including speech feedback, word prediction, pictures to write with, and the Sassoon font. OpenDyslexic is also available as an add-on.

iReadWrite font selection screen
